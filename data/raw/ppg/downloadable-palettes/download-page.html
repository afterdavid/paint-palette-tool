Steps to download PPG Colors for Photoshop:

  1. Click to save palette to Photoshop.
  2. Save file to computer.
  3. Open Photoshop application and click Swatches.
  4. Choose Load Swatches from drop down menu within swatches tab.
  5. Select downloaded document from your saved location.
